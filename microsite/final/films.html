Films

Explore the rich legacy of Studio Ghibli films, filled with magic, adventure, and timeless stories for all ages. Here are some highlights from each decade of the studio’s history.

1980s

Castle in the Sky (1986)

An epic adventure about a young boy and girl searching for a legendary floating castle in the sky.

My Neighbor Totoro (1988)

A heartwarming tale of two sisters who discover magical creatures in the forest near their home.

1990s

Princess Mononoke (1997)

A gripping story of a young warrior caught between humans and the spirits of the forest, exploring themes of nature and industrialization.

Kiki's Delivery Service (1989)

A coming-of-age story about a young witch finding her place in a new town while running her own delivery service.

2000s

Spirited Away (2001)

A breathtaking journey of a young girl trapped in a mysterious spirit world, full of magical creatures and life-changing challenges.

Howl's Moving Castle (2004)

A magical tale of love, war, and a mysterious moving castle, where a young woman must break a curse and uncover her true strength.

2010s

Ponyo (2008)

A delightful story about a goldfish who dreams of becoming human and her unlikely friendship with a little boy, blending fantasy and innocence.

When Marnie Was There (2014)

A touching mystery about a young girl’s connection to a mysterious friend, exploring themes of loneliness and belonging.
